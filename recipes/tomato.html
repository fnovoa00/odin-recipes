Baked Tomato Slices

Description

Tomatos are a delicious and nutricious fruit (... or vegetable?) that can be enjoyed as an additive to many recipes. Have you ever tried it by itself? Now with this recipe you can! Enjoy this carb-free no dough pizza recipe that does not compromize on taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • 1 garlic clove, diced
  • mozzarella (optional)
