Brazilian Brigadeiros

Description

Brigadeiros is the most popular dessert in Brazil. A treat commonly enjoyed at children's birthday parties, this low-and-slow cooked dessert is a new-to-you twist on chocolate. Have patience and fight the urge to turn up your stove heat. Form into balls, pour into small cups, or eat straight from the pan! There is no wrong way to endulge.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1 can of sweet condensed milk

Steps

  1. In a small pot in medium-low heat, add all 3 ingredients and mix
  2. Continue mixing ingredients until you can see the bottom of the pot when mixing
  3. Roll into balls (thoroughly grease hands first) once cooled
  4. Roll balls onto sprinkles
  5. Enjoy!
