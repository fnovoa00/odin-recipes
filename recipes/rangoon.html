Crab Rangoons

Description

The crab rangoon, a delicacy of the great chain of Panda Express, is my favorite dish. It is reminiscent of the Brazilian food "Pastel", which is a deep fried dough with different stuffings inside. A crab rangoon, despite its name, can be made without crab. Whatever mixture you decide to use, crab or crabless, the process is simple and the end result is mouth-watering. Just make sure to allow proper cool-down time! Your tastebuds will thank you.

Ingredients

  • wonton wrappers
  • 2 8oz packets of cream cheese
  • 1 tbsp of chopped shallots
  • 1 lb crab meat or immitation crab meat
  • 1 qt of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Thoroughly mix cream cheese, shallots, and crab together in a bowl
  2. Place roughly 1 tbsp of the mixed filling inside wonton wrappers
  3. Wet the tip of your fingers with tap water and outline the wonton wrappers
  4. Fold the wrappers by joining opposite corners together
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a pot to 350F
  6. Fry rangoons until gonden
  7. Set aside to cool
  8. Enjoy!
